Meter is the rhythmic structure of a line composed of the number of syllables and their repeating pattern.

Example(one foot):

The foot's type is determined by the repeating pattern of syllables (STRESSED or unstressed):

  • Trochaic: STRESSED-unstressed
  • Iambic: unstressed-STRESSED
  • Spondaic: STRESSED-STRESSED
  • Dactylic: STRESSED-unstressed-unstressed
  • Anapestic: unstressed-unstressed-STRESSED

The foot's repition amount is quite simple it is the word "meter" preceeded by how many time the syllable pattern is repeated using greek suffixes:

  1. Monometer
  2. Dimeter
  3. Trimeter
  4. Tetrameter
  5. Pentameter
  6. Hexameter
  7. Heptameter
  8. Octameter
